Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What if I find a recipe error?

If you notice a discrepancy between the recipe image and the text recipe, please send us an email. Include the recipe name, cook's name, and a description of the issue.

How was this recipe text created?

The recipe name, ingredients, and instructions were extracted using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology directly from the original recipe image. This ensures the authenticity of these key fields.

Other fields—such as description, notes, meta, and comments—were generated or enhanced using AI. The AI analyzes the recipe image and text to provide emphasis, clarification, highlight issues, share interesting facts, and offer modern explanations for items that may no longer be mainstream.

Most important for authenticity: The name, ingredients, and instructions are direct from the original recipe. The other fields are included to enrich your experience and understanding, but may reflect AI interpretation.

Can I share these recipes?

Absolutely! Please feel free to share the link to this website with friends and family.

How do I add missing recipes?

If you have a recipe from Fern that is not listed, please email a picture of the recipe card to recipeerror@bwalter.org with the subject line "Missing Recipe of Fern's" and it will be added promptly.

How can I make a web-based recipe box for my family?

Contact Brent at brent@bwalter.org or text (503) 855-9074 for details on how to create a digital recipe box for your loved ones.
